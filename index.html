Crime Datasets

This dataset reflects reported incidents of crime (with the exception of murders where data exists for each victim) that occurred in the City of Chicago in the years 2019, 2020 and 2021.

I wanted to see how the COVID pandemic affected crime in the city of Chicago. 1) 2019 data 2) 2020 data 3) 2021 data

2019 Data Slide

This dataset reflects reported incidents of crime (with the exception of murders where data exists for each victim) that occurred in the City of Chicago in the year of 2019. We can see that the overall rates are fairly high - particularly for battery, assault and criminal damage. Please refresh the page in between viewing the graphs.

2020 Data Slide

This dataset reflects reported incidents of crime (with the exception of murders where data exists for each victim) that occurred in the City of Chicago in the year 2020. We can see that the rates for the three highest in the prior slide (assault, battery, and criminal damage) all went down. Battery in particular went from approx 49,000 to approx 41,000. Please refresh the page in between viewing the graphs.

2021 Data Slide

This dataset reflects reported incidents of crime (with the exception of murders where data exists for each victim) that occurred in the City of Chicago in the year 2021 (so far). The graph seems to be trending normally with the assault possibly trending higher. Please refresh the page in between viewing the graphs.
